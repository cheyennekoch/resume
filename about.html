Let me introduce myself. My family and I are longtime residents of Marshall County. In 2002, I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Technology Education from Southeast Missouri State University. I then completed my Master of Arts in Secondary Education from Murray State University in 2009.

I was serving as a teacher at Marshall County High School when I felt the call into full-time ministry in 2012. Prior to beginning ministry at First Missionary Baptist Church in February 2018, I served as a student minister in the Paducah area. In 2022, I graduated from Midwestern Baptist Theological Seminary (Kansas City, MO) with a Master of Theological Studies that focused on Preaching and Pastoral Ministry.

My wife, Elyssa, is a teacher at Sharpe Elementary. We have two daughters, Adalynn and Kinley. In my spare time, I love to spend time with my wife and daughters, experiment with my grill, and enjoy the outdoors!
